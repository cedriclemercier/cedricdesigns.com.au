About Me

Hi, I’m Cedric

Before I tell you a bit more about myself, I would like to thank you for being here and reading this. Whether you are coming from a referral, from social media or even through a Google search, I assume you have a vision for your current business and want to see it come to life.

I am a freelance web designer and developer in Melbourne. I make logos, mobile app designs, animations videos and website design and development for small business in Australia, and all around the world.

I built the brand identity and websites for different companies in Australia. I also took part in several Hackathons and won most of them.

I learned how to develop solutions to business problems and add more value through beautifully crafted designs.

I have been designing since 2014 now, and still eager to learn more everyday about how I can provide more value for people I work with, whether it is in development, design, business or marketing knowledge.
